Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Overview

SmoothScroll Prompter ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. SmoothScroll Prompter is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Scripts and Content: All scripts you create are stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never have access to your content.
  • Settings: App preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking services.
  • No Advertising: We do not display ads or share data with advertisers.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your scripts sync across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud service. This data is:

  • Stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Encrypted by Apple
  • Never accessible by us

For more information about iCloud security, please refer to Apple's iCloud security overview.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive only anonymized transaction data necessary to provide premium features. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat receives anonymized subscription status data. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

All your data remains on your device and in your iCloud account. When you delete the app, your local data is removed. iCloud data can be managed through your Apple ID settings.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages as it does not collect any personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
